# Business Model, Market Opportunity & Go-To-Market Strategy

# Project

Legal TimeLock Network

---

# Executive Summary

Legal TimeLock Network is a legal-tech and blockchain infrastructure platform designed to provide immutable timestamps, digital notarization verification, tamper detection, and chain-of-custody tracking for physical legal documents.

The platform targets:

* Banks
* NBFCs
* Law Firms
* Notaries
* Courts
* Government Departments
* Citizens

---

# Business Opportunity

## Core Problem

India processes millions of:

* Property transactions
* Rental agreements
* Affidavits
* Loan agreements
* Legal contracts

every year.

Document fraud causes:

* Financial losses
* Litigation delays
* Loan fraud
* Property disputes

A trusted verification layer does not currently exist.
